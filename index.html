Midterm evaluation of reducing economic and ecological vulnerability (REEVs) project in Sool and Sanaag regions.


Over the past three years Save the Children Somaliland program has been implementing the Reduced Economic and Ecology Vulnerability (REEVs) project in Somaliland. a four-year project that is aimed at supporting drought effected and vulnerable households in target communities in Sool and Sanaag regions. The project focuses on sustainably improving community livelihoods through investing in transitional cash transfer support in the mode of both conditional and unconditional.

Client: Save Our Children
